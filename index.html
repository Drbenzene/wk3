Affordable Ride
Sharing App

Tired of paying extra when you go out? Tired of your rides
costing more than your outings? Well, Bonshare is at your service!

Traveling is already a big deal;
So, why pay more?

Bonshare is simple, safe, and affordable. We
share the costs of travelling by posting a carpool
offer on our app and connecting with other
members who are interested in taking the same
route. This way, we fill empty seats and make
transportation a better service for everyone.
